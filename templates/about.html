{{ _('Our Story') }}

Founded in 2010 by architect John Wall and master craftsman Maria Made, Wallmade began as a small workshop dedicated to creating custom architectural details for high-end residential projects. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a respected name in the architectural community.

Today, we operate from a 20,000 square foot facility with state-of-the-art equipment, but we've never lost sight of our roots in craftsmanship and attention to detail. Each piece that leaves our workshop carries the same level of care and precision as those first custom pieces we created over a decade ago.

Our team now includes architects, designers, engineers, and skilled craftsmen working together to push the boundaries of what's possible in architectural detailing.
